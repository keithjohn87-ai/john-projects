**Topic:** California Contractors: New Workers' Comp Requirements

**Post:**

```
California contractors—this one's for you.

Starting with 2026 license renewals, the CSLB will require a valid workers' compensation certificate for ALL license renewals and reinstatements.

No more "no employees" exemptions without verification.

This change (from SB 216) targets contractors who've been falsely claiming exemption while actually having employees. The state is tightening enforcement, and the penalties for non-compliance aren't small.

What you need to do now:

✅ Review your current workers' comp status
✅ If you have ANY employees (even part-time), you need coverage
✅ Get your certificate ready before your next renewal
✅ Don't wait until the last minute—processing takes time

This applies to license renewals happening in 2026 and beyond. If your renewal is coming up, start the process now.

Questions? Drop them below—happy to point you toward resources.
```
