# ContractorPro Beta Outreach Package

## Cover Letter / Email Template

---

**Subject:** Built something for contractors — need your honest take

Hey [Name],

I've been building a business system for contractors — templates, trackers, lien law guides for all 50 states. The infrastructure most of us never have time to put together.

**What's in the attached package:**
- **Document Templates:** Preliminary notices, mechanics liens, releases, contracts (HTML format, ready to use)
- **Job Costing Spreadsheet:** Track labor, materials, subs, margins per project
- **Project Tracker:** Full P&L by job with automatic margin calculation
- **AR/AP Trackers:** Know who owes you and who you owe
- **Subcontractor Tracker:** Insurance expirations, rates, performance ratings
- **COI Tracker:** Certificate of insurance monitoring
- **50-State Lien Law Database:** Deadlines, requirements, filing rules for every state

Everything's in the zip file. Use what works, ignore what doesn't.

**The feedback I need:**
1. What would you actually use in your business?
2. What's missing that you need?
3. Would you have paid for this 5 years ago? What would you pay now?

No call needed — I know you're busy. Shoot me a text or email with your thoughts whenever you have a minute.

If you hate it, tell me why. If you love it, tell me what it's worth. Either way, I owe you one.

[Your name]

P.S. — Try the Project Tracker on your current job. See if the margin calculation matches your gut feeling on profitability.

## What to Attach/Send

**Option 1: Full Professional Package (Recommended)**
- `professional-package-149.zip` (12KB)
- Include all 4 HTML templates
- Include all 4 spreadsheet trackers
- Include states.json reference

**Option 2: Custom Demo (For Big Contractors)**
- Schedule 20-min screen share
- Walk through Project Tracker with THEIR numbers
- Show 50-state database
- Let them click around live

---

## Follow-Up Strategy

**Week 1:** Send package, no pressure
**Week 2:** Casual check-in: "Did anything stand out as useful or terrible?"
**Week 3:** Ask specific question based on their business: "Would the COI tracker help with your sub management?"
**Week 4:** Close the loop: "Based on your feedback, I [made change / added feature / fixed issue]. Thanks for the help."

---

## What to Listen For

**Gold (product improvements):**
- "I wish it had X"
- "This doesn't work for my situation because..."
- "What about [specific feature]?"

**Testimonials (if they love it):**
- "This would have saved me..."
- "I wish I had this when..."
- "My [business partner/wife/accountant] would love this"

**Red flags (don't ignore):**
- "I don't really track this stuff"
- "Seems complicated"
- "I just use Excel for everything"

---

## Referral Ask (If They Love It)

"Glad it's useful. Quick favor — do you know 2-3 other contractors who might benefit from this? Not asking you to sell it, just thinking of who else deals with the same headaches."

**If they say yes:**
"Mind if I mention you reached out when I contact them? Or better yet, just intro us over email?"

---

## The Goal

Not sales. Intelligence.

You're buying feedback with free product. Their honest take is worth more than $149.

If 3 out of 5 say they'd pay for it, you have a business.
If 0 out of 5 would pay, you saved yourself months of building the wrong thing.

Either way, you win.
