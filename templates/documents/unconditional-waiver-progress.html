UNCONDITIONAL WAIVER AND RELEASE

The undersigned Claimant, having actually received and cleared the Payment Amount stated above, does hereby provide this UNCONDITIONAL WAIVER AND RELEASE ON PROGRESS PAYMENT covering the Project and Property described above.

UNCONDITIONAL NATURE OF THIS WAIVER: This waiver and release is effective immediately upon execution by the Claimant, regardless of whether the payment is subsequently stopped, reversed, or fails to clear. By signing this document, the Claimant acknowledges that payment has been received in good funds and waives all rights unconditionally.

WAIVER OF LIEN RIGHTS: The Claimant does hereby unconditionally waive, release, and relinquish any and all lien rights, payment bond rights, and other security interests that the Claimant may have or acquire against the Project, the Property, the Owner, the Contractor, and any other party with an interest in the Project, for Work performed, labor performed, materials furnished, equipment furnished, services rendered, or any other obligation related to the Project through the Effective Date stated above.

REPRESENTATIONS: The Claimant represents and warrants that:

1. The Claimant has actually received payment in the amount stated above in cleared funds;

2. The Claimant has performed Work or furnished materials, labor, or services for the Project through the Effective Date;

3. The Payment Amount represents payment for Work performed through the Effective Date;

4. The Claimant has paid or will pay from the Payment Amount all subcontractors, suppliers, and laborers for Work performed through the Effective Date for which the Claimant is responsible;

5. This waiver does not cover any retainage that may be due and owing;

6. The Claimant has the authority to execute this document and bind the Claimant's company or entity.

EXCEPTIONS - RIGHTS NOT WAIVED
IMPORTANT: This Unconditional Waiver and Release on Progress Payment does NOT waive, release, or relinquish any of the following:

1. Future Work: Any right to assert a lien or payment bond claim for Work performed after the Effective Date stated above;

2. Unpaid Retention: Any right to assert a lien or payment bond claim for unpaid retention or retainage amounts that have been earned but not yet paid;

3. Extras and Changes: Any right to assert a lien or payment bond claim for extra Work, pending modifications, change orders, or disputed claims that have not been included in this payment;

4. Pending Claims: Any pending claims, disputes, or unresolved issues related to Work performed through the Effective Date that are not covered by the Payment Amount;

5. Contractual Rights: Any rights or remedies under the Contract Documents that are not related to lien or payment bond rights.

⚠️ IMPORTANT WARNINGS:

  1. Risk of Unconditional Waiver: By signing this document, you waive your lien rights immediately—even if the payment is later stopped or reversed. Only sign after funds have cleared your account.
  2. Verify Payment First: Confirm that the payment has been received and cleared before executing this document. Do not accept this form "in exchange for" payment that has not yet been made.
  3. Consider Conditional Waiver Instead: If there is any doubt about payment clearance, use the Conditional Waiver form (G901-Style) instead.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE:

  1. When to Use: Use this form only when you have actually received and cleared payment for a progress billing.
  2. Through Date: Carefully specify the "Effective Date" or "Through Date"—this is the cutoff date for Work covered by this waiver.
  3. Exceptions Preserved: This form preserves your rights to future Work, unpaid retention, extras, and pending claims.
  4. Notarization: Some states require notarization of lien waivers—check your local requirements.
  5. State-Specific Forms: Some states have statutory lien waiver forms that must be used. This generic form may not be valid in all jurisdictions.
