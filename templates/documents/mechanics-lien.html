Filing Instructions:

  1. Complete all fields accurately. Errors can invalidate your lien.
  2. Attach required documents (varies by state):
    • Copy of contract or work order
    • Proof of preliminary notice (if required)
    • Itemized statement of amounts due
  3. File with: [County Recorder/Clerk of Court - varies by state]
  4. Filing fees: Typically $25-100 plus per-page charges
  5. Service: Most states require serving a copy on the property owner (and sometimes GC)
  6. Recordation: Get a certified copy of the recorded lien for your files

After Filing:

  • Mark your calendar for foreclosure deadline
  • Send copy to all interested parties
  • Consider settlement negotiations
  • Prepare for foreclosure action if not paid (consult attorney)
