Conditional Waiver and Release on Final Payment
(G903-Style Form)
✓ FINAL PAYMENT FORM: This form is used for the FINAL PAYMENT on a project. It covers ALL Work performed on the Project through Final Completion. This is a CONDITIONAL waiver—effective only upon actual receipt of final payment.
IMPORTANT NOTICE: This is a CONDITIONAL waiver for FINAL PAYMENT. The waiver of ALL remaining lien rights is effective only upon actual receipt of the final payment in cleared funds. Do not sign this document until you have received final payment, or if you prefer the waiver to be effective immediately upon signing, use the Unconditional Final Waiver form instead.

CONDITIONAL FINAL WAIVER AND RELEASE

The undersigned Claimant, having been paid or being entitled to be paid the Final Payment Amount stated above, does hereby provide this CONDITIONAL WAIVER AND RELEASE ON FINAL PAYMENT covering the Project and Property described above.

CONDITIONAL NATURE OF THIS WAIVER: This waiver and release is expressly conditioned upon and shall not be effective until the Claimant actually receives paid funds in the amount of the Final Payment Amount stated above. If the final payment is not received, cleared, and made available to the Claimant, this waiver shall be null and void and of no effect.

COMPLETE WAIVER OF LIEN RIGHTS: Upon the condition of actual receipt of final payment being satisfied, the Claimant does hereby waive, release, and relinquish any and all lien rights, payment bond rights, and other security interests that the Claimant may have or acquire against the Project, the Property, the Owner, the Contractor, and any other party with an interest in the Project, for ALL Work performed, labor performed, materials furnished, equipment furnished, services rendered, or any other obligation related to the Project through Final Completion.

FINAL PAYMENT COVERS ALL WORK: This waiver covers all Work performed by the Claimant on the Project from commencement through Final Completion, including but not limited to:

1. All labor, materials, equipment, and services furnished;

2. All change orders and modifications;

3. All extras and additional Work;

4. All retention and retainage amounts;

5. Any and all other claims related to the Project.

REPRESENTATIONS: The Claimant represents and warrants that:

1. The Claimant has performed all Work required by the Contract Documents through Final Completion;

2. The Final Payment Amount represents payment in full for all Work performed on the Project;

3. The Claimant has paid or will pay from the Final Payment Amount all subcontractors, suppliers, and laborers for all Work performed on the Project for which the Claimant is responsible;

4. The Claimant has no further claims against the Owner, Contractor, or Project for Work performed on this Project;

5. The Claimant has the authority to execute this document and bind the Claimant's company or entity.

EXCEPTIONS - RIGHTS NOT WAIVED
IMPORTANT: This Conditional Final Waiver does NOT waive, release, or relinquish any of the following:

1. Warranty Claims: Any rights or claims related to warranties, guarantees, or defective work that may arise after Final Completion;

2. Pending Claims: Any pending claims, disputes, or unresolved issues that have been specifically excluded by written agreement of the parties;

3. Contractual Indemnification: Any rights to indemnification or defense under the Contract Documents;

4. Mechanic's Lien for Non-Payment: If the final payment is not received and cleared, this waiver is void and the Claimant retains all lien rights.

AFFIDAVIT OF PAYMENT OF DEBTS AND CLAIMS

The undersigned Claimant hereby certifies and affirms that:

1. All subcontractors, sub-subcontractors, suppliers, and laborers who have performed Work or furnished materials, equipment, or services for the Project and for whom the Claimant is responsible have been paid in full, or will be paid in full from the Final Payment;

2. To the best of the Claimant's knowledge, there are no outstanding claims, liens, or security interests against the Project by any party for whom the Claimant is responsible;

3. The Claimant has complied with all applicable laws regarding the payment of wages and benefits to employees;

4. The Claimant has obtained all required lien waivers from subcontractors and suppliers.

INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE:

  1. When to Use: Use this form when you have received or are about to receive the FINAL PAYMENT on a project and want to conditionally waive ALL remaining lien rights.
  2. Final Payment Only: This form covers ALL Work on the Project. Do not use for progress payments—use G901 or G902 instead.
  3. Conditional Nature: This waiver is NOT effective until final payment is actually received and cleared. If the check bounces or payment fails, the waiver is void.
  4. Complete Release: This form waives ALL lien rights for the Project, including retention and change orders.
  5. Affidavit Included: This form includes an affidavit of payment—use it to certify that all subs and suppliers have been paid.
  6. Notarization: Some states require notarization of final lien waivers—check your local requirements.
  7. State-Specific Forms: Some states have statutory lien waiver forms that must be used. This generic form may not be valid in all jurisdictions.
