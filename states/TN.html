Tennessee Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Tennessee.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Tennessee Board for Licensing Contractors

Visit Board Website →

Key Requirements:

  • License Threshold: $25,000+ for prime contractors
  • Exams: Trade and business law exams required
  • Bond: $500,000 limit or less requires bond
  • Insurance: General liability insurance required

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Notice to Owner required before commencing work for subcontractors

Filing Deadline: Within 1 year of last furnishing labor or materials

Foreclosure Period: Within 90 days of serving lien on owner, or 1 year from filing

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 7% state + up to 2.75% local tax

Contractor Tax Notes: Contractors pay sales tax on materials; installation labor generally not taxable
