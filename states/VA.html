Virginia Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Virginia.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Virginia Department of Professional and Occupational Regulation (DPOR)

Visit DPOR Website →

Key Requirements:

  • License Classes: Class A ($120K+), Class B ($10K-$120K), Class C (under $10K)
  • Exams: Business and trade exams required for Class A and B
  • Designated Employee: Must have a qualified individual
  • Insurance: General liability insurance required

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Not required for prime contractors

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days from last day of month when work/materials were last furnished

Foreclosure Period: Within 6 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 4.3% state + 1% mandatory local = 5.3% (some areas higher)

Contractor Tax Notes: Contractors pay sales tax on materials. Effective 2017, contractors who sell and install certain items are treated as traditional contractors for tax purposes.
