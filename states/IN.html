Indiana Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Indiana.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Indiana Professional Licensing Agency (IPLA)

No statewide general contractor license required

Visit IPLA Website →

Key Requirements:

  • General Contractors: No state license required
  • Local Licensing: Required by most cities/counties
  • Plumbing: State license required through IPLA
  • Electrical: State license required through IPLA
  • HVAC: State license required through IPLA

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Residential Projects (1-2 dwellings): Within 60 days of last work

Commercial Projects: Within 90 days of last work

Foreclosure Period: Within 1 year of filing lien

⚠️ Important: File a "Sworn Statement and Notice of Intention to Hold Mechanic's Lien" with the county recorder. Deadlines differ based on project type.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 7% state rate

Local Rates: No local sales tax in Indiana

Contractor Tax Notes: Contractors are considered retail merchants and must collect sales tax on construction services. Materials purchased are tax-exempt with resale certificate.
