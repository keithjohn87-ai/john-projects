South Carolina Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in South Carolina.

📋 Licensing Requirements

South Carolina Contractor's Licensing Board

Visit Board Website →

Key Requirements:

  • License Threshold: $5,000+ for residential, $50,000+ for commercial
  • Exams: Technical and business law exams required
  • Bond: Surety bond required based on license type
  • Insurance: General liability insurance required

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Not required for prime contractors

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing labor or materials

Foreclosure Period: Within 6 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 6% state + up to 1% local option tax

Contractor Tax Notes: Contractors pay sales tax on materials purchased; no resale certificate for construction materials
