Nevada Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Nevada.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Nevada State Contractors Board

Visit Board Website →

Key Requirements:

  • License Required: For all construction work over $1,000
  • Exams: Trade and business management exams required
  • Experience: 4 years journeyman-level experience required
  • Bond: License bond required (amount varies by classification)

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Notice of Intent: Must be served at least 15 days before recording lien

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of work stoppage or completion

Enforcement Deadline: Within 6 months of filing notice or acceptance of work (whichever is earlier)

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 6.85% base rate + local taxes

Contractor Tax Notes: Contractors are consumers of materials used in real property improvements. Pay sales tax on materials purchased; do not collect sales tax from customers on construction contracts.
