North Dakota Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in North Dakota.

📋 Licensing Requirements

North Dakota Secretary of State - Contractor Registration

Visit Registration Website →

Key Requirements:

  • General Contractors: No state license required
  • Trade Licenses: Electrical, plumbing, HVAC require state licensing
  • Registration: Required with Secretary of State
  • Workers' Comp: Required through Workforce Safety & Insurance

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Notice of Intent: At least 10 days before filing lien

Lien Filing: Within 90 days of last furnishing

Late Filing: Allowed up to 3 years with limited protections

Enforcement: Within 3 years of filing, or 30 days after written demand

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 5% state rate

Contractor Tax Notes: Contractors pay sales tax on materials purchased. Use tax applies to out-of-state purchases. Tangible personal property vs. real property distinctions apply.
