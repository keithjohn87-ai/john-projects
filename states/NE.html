Nebraska Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Nebraska.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Nebraska Department of Labor - Contractor Registration

Visit Registration Website →

Key Requirements:

  • Registration Threshold: Required for contracts over $2,500
  • Registration Fee: $40.00
  • Bond: Required based on project size
  • Insurance: Workers' compensation required

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Not required for prime contractors

Filing Deadline: Within 120 days of last furnishing labor or materials

Notice to Owner: Must serve stamped copy within 10 days of filing

Foreclosure Period: Within 2 years of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 5.5% state + local rates (average 1.4%)

Contractor Tax Notes: Contractors have three tax options (Option 1, 2, or 3). Option 1 allows tax-free inventory with sales tax collected from customers on materials.
