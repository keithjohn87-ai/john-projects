Missouri Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Missouri.

📋 Licensing Requirements

No Statewide General Contractor License

Licensing handled at local level

Visit Missouri Website →

Key Requirements:

  • General Contractors: No state license required
  • Local Licensing: Required by most cities/counties
  • Electrical: Local licensing typically required
  • Plumbing/HVAC: Local licensing typically required
  • Insurance: Varies by municipality

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Not required for prime contractors

Filing Deadline: Within 6 months of last furnishing labor/materials

Equipment Lessors: Within 60 days of equipment leaving site

Foreclosure Period: Within 6 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missouri mechanics' liens cannot be extended. Must file lawsuit to enforce within 6 months of filing lien.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 4.225% state rate

Local Rates: Additional local taxes may apply

Contractor Tax Notes: Contractors pay sales tax on materials. Construction labor is generally exempt from sales tax.
