Idaho Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Idaho.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Idaho Contractors Board

Division of Occupational and Professional Licenses (DOPL)

Visit Board Website →

Key Requirements:

  • Registration: Required for all contractors
  • Application Fee: $50 for registration
  • Insurance: Minimum $300,000 general liability
  • Workers' Comp: Required if you have employees
  • Reciprocity: Available with UT, AZ, OR, CA, FL

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Not required for prime contractors

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days after substantial completion (not including punch list or warranty work)

Foreclosure Period: Within 6 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: File a "Claim of Lien" with the county recorder. Extensions are not available in Idaho.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 6% state rate

Local Rates: Additional local option taxes may apply

Contractor Tax Notes: Contractors are considered consumers of materials and pay sales tax on purchases. No resale certificate for construction materials.
