# CONTRACTOR MARKETING PLAYBOOK

## Lead Generation Strategies That Actually Work

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## 1. REFERRAL SYSTEM (Highest ROI)

**Why it works:** People trust recommendations from friends/family.

**How to implement:**
- Ask every satisfied customer for referrals
- Offer incentives: $100-500 for signed contracts
- Create referral cards customers can hand out
- Follow up with past customers quarterly

**Script:**
"I'm glad you're happy with the work. Do you know anyone else who might need [service]? I'd appreciate the referral and will take great care of them."

**Target:** 30-50% of leads from referrals

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## 2. GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE (Free & Essential)

**Setup:**
- Claim your Google Business listing
- Add photos of completed projects (before/after)
- Respond to ALL reviews (good and bad)
- Post updates weekly
- Add Q&A section with common questions

**Optimization:**
- Use keywords in description: "[City] roofing contractor"
- Add services with descriptions
- Upload video walkthroughs
- Encourage reviews from every customer

**Goal:** Top 3 local map results

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## 3. WEBSITE ESSENTIALS

**Must-Haves:**
- Mobile-friendly design
- Clear call-to-action: "Get Free Estimate"
- Phone number in header (click-to-call)
- Photo gallery of work
- Customer testimonials
- License/insurance info
- Service area map

**Pages to Include:**
- Home (benefits-focused)
- Services (one page per service)
- About/Team
- Portfolio/Gallery
- Reviews/Testimonials
- Contact/Estimate Request

**SEO Basics:**
- Page titles with location: "Roofing Contractor in [City]"
- Meta descriptions
- Alt text on images
- Local schema markup
- Fast loading speed

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## 4. PAID ADVERTISING (When Ready)

**Google Ads:**
- Target: "[service] + [city]" keywords
- Budget: Start with $500-1000/month
- Track cost per lead religiously
- Pause underperforming keywords

**Facebook/Instagram:**
- Target homeowners in your area
- Use before/after photos
- Video content performs best
- Retarget website visitors

**Lead Services (Use Cautiously):**
- HomeAdvisor, Angi, Thumbtack
- High cost per lead
- Quality varies
- Negotiate rates

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## 5. SOCIAL MEDIA FOR CONTRACTORS

**Best Platforms:**
1. Facebook (homeowners 35-65)
2. Instagram (visual portfolio)
3. Nextdoor (hyperlocal)

**Content Ideas:**
- Before/after project photos
- Time-lapse videos
- Customer testimonials
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Educational tips
- Team spotlights
- Community involvement

**Posting Schedule:**
- Facebook: 3-4x per week
- Instagram: Daily stories, 3x weekly posts
- Nextdoor: Weekly helpful posts (not salesy)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## 6. NETWORKING & PARTNERSHIPS

**Target Partners:**
- Real estate agents
- Property managers
- Insurance agents
- Other contractors (non-competing)
- Architects/designers
- Suppliers

**Approach:**
- Offer reciprocal referrals
- Provide excellent service on their projects
- Stay top-of-mind with quarterly check-ins
- Join local business groups (BNI, Chamber)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## 7. REVIEW MANAGEMENT

**Why Reviews Matter:**
- 90%+ of customers read reviews before calling
- Top factor in local search ranking
- Builds trust before you meet

**Getting Reviews:**
- Ask immediately after completion
- Send follow-up email with link
- Make it easy (one-click)
- Respond to every review within 24 hours

**Platforms (in order of importance):**
1. Google
2. Facebook
3. Yelp
4. Industry-specific (HomeAdvisor, etc.)

**Goal:** 10+ new reviews per month

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## 8. TRACKING & METRICS

**Track Monthly:**
- Total leads
- Leads by source
- Cost per lead by source
- Conversion rate (lead to sale)
- Average job value
- Customer acquisition cost

**Tools:**
- Google Analytics (website)
- Call tracking numbers
- CRM spreadsheet
- Simple dashboard

**Decision Rule:**
Double down on what works. Cut what doesn't.

## MARKETING BUDGET GUIDELINES

**Startup (Under $500K revenue):**
- 5-10% of revenue on marketing
- Focus: Referrals, Google, basic website

**Growth ($500K-$2M):**
- 3-5% of revenue
- Add: Paid ads, social media, content

**Established ($2M+):**
- 2-3% of revenue
- Focus: Brand building, systems, scale
