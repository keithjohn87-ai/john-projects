## 💼 ACCOUNTS RECEIVABLE SYSTEM

### The Collection Schedule

**Day 7:** Friendly reminder email
**Day 15:** Phone call
**Day 30:** Past due notice
**Day 45:** Second call + email
**Day 60:** Demand letter
**Day 75:** Final notice before lien
**Day 90:** File mechanics lien
**Day 120:** Collections agency

### Templates Included:
1. Friendly Reminder (7 days)
2. Past Due Notice (30 days)
3. Demand Letter (60 days)
4. Final Notice Before Lien (75 days)
5. Notice of Intent to Lien (90 days)

### Key Metrics to Track:
- Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)
- Collection rate by client
- Average collection time
- Bad debt percentage

**Target:** DSO under 45 days

## 💰 CASH FLOW PROJECTION

### 13-Week Rolling Forecast

**Why 13 weeks?**
- Covers one full quarter
- Long enough to see problems coming
- Short enough to be accurate

**What to Include:**
- Known receivables (by expected payment date)
- Known payables (payroll, subs, suppliers)
- Loan payments
- Equipment purchases
- Tax deposits
- Owner draws

**Update Weekly:**
- Actual collections vs projected
- New payables
- Change orders received
- New proposals pending
