## Document Checklist for Financial Projections

**Historical Financials (3 years):**
- [ ] Personal tax returns
- [ ] Business tax returns
- [ ] Year-end financial statements
- [ ] Current financial statements (last 90 days)

**Supporting Documentation:**
- [ ] Bank statements (last 6 months)
- [ ] A/R aging report
- [ ] A/P aging report
- [ ] Equipment list with values
- [ ] Major contracts in hand
- [ ] Pending bids/proposals
- [ ] Lease agreements
- [ ] Loan statements (existing debt)

**Projection Support:**
- [ ] Market research data
- [ ] Industry benchmarks
- [ ] Customer letters of intent
- [ ] Signed contracts
- [ ] Equipment quotes
- [ ] Real estate appraisals

---

**Remember:** SBA lenders want to see realistic, conservative projections. It's better to under-promise and over-deliver than to project unrealistic growth.
