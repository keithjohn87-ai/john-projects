## 📋 FORM-BY-FORM GUIDE

### Preliminary Notice
**When to use:** Within your state's deadline (typically 10-30 days from first work)
**Why it matters:** Without this, you may lose lien rights
**How to send:** Certified mail with return receipt

### Mechanics Lien
**When to use:** When you haven't been paid and the deadline is approaching
**Warning:** Strict deadlines apply - don't wait
**Filing:** County recorder's office where property is located

### Release of Lien
**When to use:** After receiving payment
**Types:** Conditional (if payment pending) vs Unconditional (payment received)
**Never sign:** Until payment clears your bank

### Change Order
**When to use:** Any time scope, price, or schedule changes
**Rule:** No work on changed items until signed by both parties

### Job Costing Calculator
**When to use:** For every bid
**Tracks:** Labor, materials, equipment, overhead, profit
**Result:** Accurate pricing that ensures profitability
