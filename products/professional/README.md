## 💰 SBA FUNDING GUIDE HIGHLIGHTS

### Loan Types Explained:

**7(a) Loan** (Up to $5M)
- Working capital
- Equipment purchases
- Business acquisition
- Refinancing debt

**504 Loan** (Up to $5.5M)
- Commercial real estate
- Heavy equipment
- Long-term fixed assets

**Microloan** (Up to $50K)
- Working capital
- Inventory
- Supplies
- Equipment

### What You'll Need:
- Personal financial statement
- Business tax returns (3 years)
- Personal tax returns (3 years)
- Business plan
- Financial projections
- Collateral documentation
- Resumes of key owners

### Timeline:
- Pre-qualification: 1-2 weeks
- Full application: 2-4 weeks
- Approval: 30-60 days
- Funding: 60-90 days total
