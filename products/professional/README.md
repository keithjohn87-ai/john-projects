## How to Use

### HTML Forms
1. Open in browser
2. Fill in fields
3. Print or save as PDF

### CSV Spreadsheets
1. Open in Excel, Google Sheets, or Numbers
2. Enable editing
3. Enter your data
4. Save with your company name
