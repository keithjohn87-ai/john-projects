# Construction Website Template - Market Research

## Competitor Analysis & Pricing

### Premium Template Marketplaces

#### ThemeForest (Envato Market)
**Typical Pricing:**
- Standard License: $29-59
- Extended License: $500-2000
- Popular construction templates: $39-49

**Top Performers:**
- Construction Pro themes: $39-59
- Builder/contractor focused: $29-49
- Multi-purpose with construction demos: $59

#### Creative Market
**Typical Pricing:**
- Single template: $19-39
- Bundle (3-5 templates): $49-99
- Construction niche: $24-49

#### TemplateMonster
**Typical Pricing:**
- Standard: $29-59
- Premium with builder: $69-99
- Yearly subscription: $149/year unlimited

### Pricing Strategy Recommendations

#### Option 1: Single Template Model
- **Price:** $49
- **Positioning:** Premium, professional
- **Includes:** Homepage, Services, Gallery, About, Contact

#### Option 2: Bundle Model
- **Single Template:** $39
- **3-Pack Bundle:** $89 (save $28)
- **5-Pack Bundle:** $129 (save $66)

#### Option 3: Tiered Model
- **Basic:** $29 (homepage + services + contact)
- **Professional:** $49 (all pages + animations)
- **Premium:** $79 (all pages + customization service)

### Competitive Advantages for TradeElegance

1. **Elegant Design** - Most construction templates are utilitarian/ugly
2. **Industry Authenticity** - Built by someone with construction background
3. **Mobile-First** - Many older templates aren't properly responsive
4. **Fast Loading** - Optimized code vs bloated WordPress themes
5. **Customization Service** - Upsell to customize for their business

### Market Positioning

**Target Price Point:** $49-59
- Premium but accessible
- Justified by quality and uniqueness
- Room for bundle discounts

### Distribution Channels

1. **Direct Sales (Highest Margin)**
   - Own website: TradeElegance.com
   - Keep 100% of revenue
   - Gumroad/Stripe integration

2. **Marketplaces (Lower Margin, Higher Volume)**
   - Creative Market: 30% commission
   - ThemeForest: 30-50% commission
   - TemplateMonster: 30% commission

### Recommended Launch Strategy

1. **Phase 1:** Direct sales only (build brand)
2. **Phase 2:** Add Creative Market (reach designers)
3. **Phase 3:** Add ThemeForest (highest volume)

### Price Testing

Start at $49, test:
- $39 (if conversion low)
- $59 (if conversion high)
- Bundle deals for volume

## Notes
- Most construction companies will pay $50-150 for a quality template
- Custom website development costs $2,000-10,000
- Template is 90% cheaper with 80% of the value
- Focus on ROI: "Professional website for less than a day's labor"
