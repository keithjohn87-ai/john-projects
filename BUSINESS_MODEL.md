## Marketing Blueprint

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)

**Goals:**
- Set up tracking and analytics
- Create core content
- Build initial email list

**Tactics:**
1. **SEO Content** (Free, long-term)
   - Blog posts: "How to get licensed in [State]"
   - Target: "[State] contractor license requirements"
   - 1 post per week, 50 states over time
   - Cost: $0 (DIY writing)

2. **Email Capture** (Free)
   - Lead magnet: "Free Preliminary Notice Template"
   - Exit-intent popup on site
   - Cost: $0

3. **Google Business Profile** (Free)
   - List as digital product business
   - Target local contractors
   - Cost: $0

**Budget:** $0  
**Expected Result:** 100-200 email subscribers

---

### Phase 2: Traction (Month 3-6)

**Goals:**
- Drive targeted traffic
- Get first 50 customers
- Build testimonials

**Tactics:**
1. **Facebook/Instagram Ads** ($300/month)
   - Target: Contractors, construction business owners
   - Age: 30-55
   - Interests: Construction, contracting, small business
   - Ad creative: Problem/solution ("Stop losing money to lien mistakes")
   - Expected CPC: $1-2
   - Expected conversion: 1-2%
   - Expected ROAS: 2-3x

2. **LinkedIn Ads** ($200/month)
   - Target: Construction company owners, general contractors
   - Job titles: Owner, President, CEO, Project Manager
   - Higher CPC ($3-5) but better quality
   - Expected ROAS: 3-4x

3. **Reddit Marketing** (Free)
   - r/Construction, r/Contractors, r/SmallBusiness
   - Answer questions, provide value
   - Soft mention of product when relevant
   - Cost: $0

4. **Quora** (Free)
   - Answer licensing/lien law questions
   - Link to relevant state pages
   - Build authority
   - Cost: $0

**Budget:** $500/month  
**Expected Result:** 500-1,000 monthly visitors, 10-20 sales/month

---

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 7-12)

**Goals:**
- 50+ sales/month
- Optimize for acquisition
- Build case studies

**Tactics:**
1. **Google Ads** ($500/month)
   - Target: High-intent keywords
   - "Contractor license requirements [State]"
   - "Mechanics lien form [State]"
   - "SBA loan for contractors"
   - Expected CPC: $2-4
   - Expected conversion: 2-3%

2. **Retargeting** ($200/month)
   - Facebook/Instagram pixel
   - Target: Visitors who didn't buy
   - Offer: Limited-time discount or bonus
   - Expected lift: 20-30% additional sales

3. **Partnerships** (Free/Rev share)
   - Construction insurance agents
   - Surety bond companies
   - Contractor associations
   - Offer: 20% commission on referrals
   - Cost: $0 upfront

4. **Affiliate Program** (Rev share only)
   - Construction bloggers, YouTubers
   - 20-30% commission
   - Cost: $0 upfront

5. **Content SEO** (Continue)
   - Now have 20-30 blog posts
   - Organic traffic growing
   - Cost: $0

**Budget:** $700/month  
**Expected Result:** 2,000+ monthly visitors, 40-60 sales/month

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|------------|------------|
| Low traffic | Medium | Start with free SEO/content, add paid slowly |
| Low conversion | Medium | A/B test pricing, improve sales copy |
| High refunds | Low | Clear product description, good support docs |
| Competition | Medium | Focus on 50-state depth, hard to replicate |
| Platform risk (Google/Facebook bans) | Low | Diversify traffic sources, own email list |
