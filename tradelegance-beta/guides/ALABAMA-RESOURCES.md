## QUICK REFERENCE - TAX RATES

### Sales Tax (Materials)
- State: 4%
- Jefferson County (Birmingham): +2% = 6%
- Montgomery County: +2.5% = 6.5%
- Mobile County: +3% = 7%
- Madison County (Huntsville): +2% = 6%

### Income Tax
- Graduated rate: 2% - 5%
- Corporate: 6.5% (2026)
