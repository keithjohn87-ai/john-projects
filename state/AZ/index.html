Arizona Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Arizona.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Arizona Registrar of Contractors

Visit Board Website →

Key Requirements:

  • License required for projects over $1,000
  • Pass trade exam and business management exam
  • Bond required ($4,250 - $15,000 based on classification)
  • Workers' compensation insurance required

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Required within 20 days of first furnishing

Filing Deadline: Within 120 days of completion

Foreclosure Period: Within 6 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 5.6% state + local rates

Contractor Tax Notes: Prime contractors exempt from sales tax on materials
