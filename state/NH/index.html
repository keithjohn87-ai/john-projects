New Hampshire Contractor Resources

📋 Licensing

New Hampshire Office of Professional Licensure and Certification

Official Licensing Board →

Requirements:

  • No state-level general contractor license
  • Plumbing and electrical state-licensed
  • Home improvement contractors must register
  • Local jurisdictions may require licensing

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Account within 120 days of last furnishing (subcontractors)

Filing Deadline: Within 120 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 1 year of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Verify current New Hampshire lien law requirements as they may change.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: No state sales tax

Contractor Tax: Business profits tax and interest/dividends tax may apply
