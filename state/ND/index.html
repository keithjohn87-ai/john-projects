North Dakota Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in North Dakota.

📋 Licensing Requirements

North Dakota Secretary of State

Visit Board Website →

Key Requirements:

  • No state-level general contractor license
  • Plumbing and electrical state-licensed
  • Contractor registration required
  • Local jurisdictions may require additional licensing

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Not required

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing (residential: 60 days)

Foreclosure Period: Within 2 years of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 5% state + local rates

Contractor Tax Notes: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
