📋 Licensing

No state-level licensing (local jurisdictions)

Visit MO.gov →

Requirements:

  • No state-level general contractor license
  • Local jurisdictions set their own requirements
  • Kansas City and St. Louis require licenses
  • Electrical, plumbing, and HVAC state-licensed
  • Insurance typically required locally

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Intent to Lien required 10 days before filing

Filing Deadline: Within 6 months of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 6 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missouri requires a 10-day Notice of Intent before filing lien. Don't skip this step.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 4.225% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
