Missouri Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Missouri.

📋 Licensing Requirements

No state-level licensing (local jurisdictions)

Visit State Website →

Key Requirements:

  • No state-level general contractor license
  • Local jurisdictions set their own requirements
  • Kansas City and St. Louis require licenses
  • Electrical, plumbing, and HVAC state-licensed
  • Insurance typically required locally

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Intent to Lien required 10 days before filing

Filing Deadline: Within 6 months of last furnishing

Foreclosure Period: Within 6 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 4.225% state + local rates

Contractor Tax Notes: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
