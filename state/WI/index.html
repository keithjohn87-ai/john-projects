⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Intent to File Claim of Lien at least 30 days before filing

Filing Deadline: Within 6 months of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 2 years of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Wisconsin requires a 30-day Notice of Intent before filing. This is one of the longest notice periods in the country.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 5% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
