⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Unpaid Balance required within 60 days for residential

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 1 year of filing lien

⚠️ Important: NJ has strict residential lien requirements. The Notice of Unpaid Balance must be filed within 60 days.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 6.625% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
