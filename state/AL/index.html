Alabama Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Alabama.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Alabama Licensing Board for General Contractors

Visit Board Website →

Key Requirements:

  • Projects over $50,000 require license
  • Pass trade exam and business law exam
  • Proof of insurance required
  • Financial statement required for projects over $150,000

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Not required for prime contractors

Filing Deadline: Within 6 months of last furnishing labor/materials

Foreclosure Period: Within 6 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 4% state + local rates

Contractor Tax Notes: Use tax may apply on materials
