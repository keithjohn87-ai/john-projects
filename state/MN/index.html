⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Preliminary notice recommended but not required

Filing Deadline: Within 120 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 1 year of last furnishing

⚠️ Important: Minnesota's foreclosure deadline is calculated from last furnishing, not lien filing. Track your dates carefully.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 6.875% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
