Requirements:

  • No state-level general contractor license
  • Chicago requires General Contractor license
  • Cook County requires contractor registration
  • Specialty trades (electrical, plumbing) state-licensed
  • Local jurisdictions set their own requirements

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Intent to Lien required within 90 days of last furnishing (subcontractors/suppliers)

Filing Deadline: Within 4 months of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 2 years of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Illinois has strict notice requirements. Subcontractors must send Notice of Intent to Lien before filing.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 6.25% state + local rates (up to 11% in Chicago)

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt for new construction
