Mississippi Contractor Resources

📋 Licensing

Mississippi State Board of Contractors

Official Licensing Board →

Requirements:

  • Commercial license required for projects over $50,000
  • Residential license required for projects over $10,000
  • Pass Mississippi Law and Business Management Exam
  • Surety bond required
  • Insurance required

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Intent required 10 days before filing (subcontractors)

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 1 year of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Verify current Mississippi lien law requirements as they may change.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 7% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
