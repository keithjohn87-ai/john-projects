Requirements:

  • No state-level general contractor license
  • Home improvement contractors must register
  • Specialty trades (HVAC, electrical, plumbing, hydronics) state-licensed
  • Local jurisdictions may require registration
  • Must pass Ohio Home Improvement Contractor Exam for HIC

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Furnishing required within 21 days of first furnishing (subcontractors/suppliers)

Filing Deadline: Within 75 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 6 years of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Ohio has a unique 21-day Notice of Furnishing requirement. Missing this deadline can invalidate lien rights for subcontractors.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 5.75% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
