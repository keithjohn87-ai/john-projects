⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice to Owner/Contractor required within 30 days for some claimants

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 365 days of filing lien (must file commencement of action within 30 days of suit)

⚠️ Important: Georgia requires a "Commencement of Lien Action" notice filed within 30 days of filing suit.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 4% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally not taxable
