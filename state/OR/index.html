Oregon Contractor Resources

📋 Licensing

Oregon Construction Contractors Board

Official Licensing Board →

Requirements:

  • Residential or Commercial license required
  • Pass CCB exam
  • Surety bond required ($15,000-$20,000)
  • Insurance required
  • 16 hours of pre-license education

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Right to Lien within 60 days of first furnishing

Filing Deadline: Within 75 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 1 year of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Verify current Oregon lien law requirements as they may change.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: No state sales tax

Contractor Tax: Construction excise tax may apply in some jurisdictions
