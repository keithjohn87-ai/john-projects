Virginia Contractor Resources

📋 Licensing

Virginia Department of Professional and Occupational Regulation (DPOR)

Visit DPOR →

Requirements:

  • Class A: Projects over $120,000; Class B: $10K-$120K; Class C: under $10K
  • 8 hours pre-license education required
  • Must pass Business Law exam
  • Trade exam may be required depending on classification
  • General liability and workers' comp insurance required

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice to Owner required at time of filing for subcontractors

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 6 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Virginia has a short 6-month foreclosure window — one of the shortest in the nation. Act quickly if payment is not received.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 5.3% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
