Washington Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Washington.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Washington State Department of Labor & Industries

Visit Board Website →

Key Requirements:

  • Registration required for any work over $500
  • General Contractor or Specialty Contractor registration
  • Surety bond required ($12,000 for general contractors)
  • General liability insurance required
  • Workers' compensation required if employees

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Model Disclosure Statement required for residential; Notice to Owner within 60 days for commercial (subcontractors)

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure Period: Within 8 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 6.5% state + local rates

Contractor Tax Notes: Retail sales tax applies to construction services
