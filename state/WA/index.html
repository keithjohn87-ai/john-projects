Requirements:

  • Registration required for any work over $500
  • General Contractor or Specialty Contractor registration
  • Surety bond required ($12,000 for general contractors)
  • General liability insurance required
  • Workers' compensation required if employees

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Model Disclosure Statement required for residential; Notice to Owner within 60 days for commercial (subcontractors)

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 8 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Washington requires a specific "Model Disclosure Statement" for residential work. Subcontractors must send Notice to Owner within 60 days.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 6.5% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Retail sales tax applies to construction services
