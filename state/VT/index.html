Vermont Contractor Resources

📋 Licensing

Vermont Department of Public Safety

Official Licensing Board →

Requirements:

  • No state-level general contractor license
  • Plumbing and electrical state-licensed
  • Local jurisdictions may require licensing
  • Business registration required

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Right to Lien within 30 days of first furnishing (subcontractors)

Filing Deadline: Within 180 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 1 year of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Verify current Vermont lien law requirements as they may change.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 6% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
