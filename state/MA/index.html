Massachusetts Contractor Resources

Complete guide to licensing, lien laws, and compliance for contractors in Massachusetts.

📋 Licensing Requirements

Massachusetts Office of Consumer Affairs and Business Regulation

Visit Board Website →

Key Requirements:

  • Construction Supervisor License (CSL) required
  • Home Improvement Contractor (HIC) registration required
  • Must pass exam for CSL
  • 3 years experience required for CSL
  • Boston requires additional city license

⚖️ Lien Law Deadlines

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Contract required for residential (record within 30 days of contract)

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing (residential) or 60 days (commercial)

Foreclosure Period: Within 90 days of recording Statement of Account

⚠️ Important: Missing lien deadlines can result in loss of payment rights. Always document your work and serve notices promptly.

💰 Tax Information

Sales Tax Rate: 6.25% state + local rates

Contractor Tax Notes: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
