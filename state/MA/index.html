⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Contract required for residential (record within 30 days of contract)

Filing Deadline: Within 90 days of last furnishing (residential) or 60 days (commercial)

Foreclosure: Within 90 days of recording Statement of Account

⚠️ Important: Massachusetts has complex lien requirements. The Notice of Contract must be recorded early in residential projects.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 6.25% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
