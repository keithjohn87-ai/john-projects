📋 Licensing

Requirements:

  • No state-level general contractor license
  • Local jurisdictions set their own requirements
  • Indianapolis requires contractor license
  • Plumbing, HVAC, and electrical state-licensed
  • Insurance and bonding typically required locally

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Notice of Intent to File Lien may be sent

Filing Deadline: Within 60 days (residential) or 90 days (commercial) of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 1 year of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Indiana has different deadlines for residential vs commercial projects. Know which applies to your job.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 7% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor generally exempt
