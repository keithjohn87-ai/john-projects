Arkansas Contractor Resources

📋 Licensing

Arkansas Contractors Licensing Board

Official Licensing Board →

Requirements:

  • License required for commercial projects over $50,000
  • Residential builders must register
  • Pass Arkansas Business and Law Exam
  • Financial statement required
  • Insurance and bond required

⚖️ Lien Laws

Preliminary Notice: Not required for prime contractors

Filing Deadline: Within 120 days of last furnishing

Foreclosure: Within 15 months of filing lien

⚠️ Important: Verify current Arkansas lien law requirements as they may change.

💰 Taxes

Sales Tax: 6.5% state + local rates

Contractor Tax: Materials taxable; labor exempt for new construction
