## 💰 Owner Financing — What to Negotiate

### Key Terms to Protect

| Term | Good | Red Flag |
|------|------|----------|
| **Interest rate** | 7-9% or below market | 10%+ with no negotiation |
| **Down payment** | 10-20% | 30%+ |
| **Loan term** | 15-30 years | Balloon payment in 5-7 years |
| **Prepayment** | Allowed anytime | Penalty for early payoff |
| **Default clause** | Grace period + cure options | Immediate foreclosure |
| **Escrow** | None or minimal | High escrow requirements |

### Red Flag Contracts
- **Balloon payments** — owe full balance in 5-7 years
- **High prepayment penalties** — lock you in
- **Aggressive default terms** — sellerforecloses fast
- **No warranty of title** — risk of liens

### Pro Tips
- Get **title insurance** (even with owner finance)
- **Negotiate interest rate** — sellers often have flexibility
- Request **grace period** (15-30 days) for late payments
- **Consult a real estate attorney** — $500-1K now saves headaches later
