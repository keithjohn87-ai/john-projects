# Wife Training Guide - Customer Support

## Role Overview
Handle exceptions only:
- Refund requests
- Technical issues (download problems)
- Custom document requests
- Angry/upset customers

## What NOT to Handle
- Routine questions → Direct to FAQ
- Sales inquiries → Direct to website
- Feature requests → Log and escalate to John

## Response Templates

### Refund Request (Within 30 days)
```
Hi [Name],

I'm sorry the product didn't meet your expectations. I can process a full refund for you.

It will appear on your card within 5-7 business days.

Is there anything specific that didn't work for you? Your feedback helps us improve.

Best,
[Her name]
ContractorPro Support
```

### Technical Issue (Can't Download)
```
Hi [Name],

I'm sorry you're having trouble accessing your files. Let me help.

Can you tell me:
1. What error message are you seeing?
2. Are you on mobile or computer?
3. What browser are you using?

I'll send you a fresh download link right away.

Best,
[Her name]
```

### Angry Customer
```
Hi [Name],

I understand your frustration, and I sincerely apologize for [specific issue].

Here's what I'm going to do to fix this:
[Specific action]

I appreciate your patience. Please let me know if there's anything else I can do.

Best,
[Her name]
```

### Custom Request (Document modification)
```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out. Custom document modifications are handled on a case-by-case basis.

Can you tell me more about what you need? I'll review and get back to you within 24 hours with options and pricing.

Best,
[Her name]
```

## Escalation Rules

**Escalate to John immediately if:**
- Legal threat
- Chargeback notification
- Request for partnership/reseller
- Media inquiry
- Anything involving IP/patents

**Log but don't escalate:**
- Feature requests (weekly summary to John)
- Common questions (update FAQ)
- Positive feedback (share with team)

## Tools Access

**She needs:**
- Access to CharlesCreatorAI@gmail.com
- Stripe dashboard (read-only)
- Telegram for Charles questions
- Google Drive (customer records)

## Training Schedule

**Week 1:** Shadow mode
- She watches John handle 5-10 support emails
- Reads all templates
- Asks questions

**Week 2:** Assisted mode
- She drafts responses
- John reviews before sending
- Feedback loop

**Week 3:** Independent mode
- She handles routine issues
- Escalates exceptions
- Weekly check-in with John

## Success Metrics
- Response time: < 4 hours during business hours
- Refund rate: < 5%
- Customer satisfaction: Track via follow-up emails
- Escalation rate: < 10% of tickets
