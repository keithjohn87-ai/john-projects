## Mission
Build a construction business framework that:
1. Serves established contractors (large residential/commercial)
2. Generates revenue with minimal ongoing management
3. Becomes an attractive acquisition target for strategic buyers

---

## Product Structure

### Pricing Tiers
1. **Essential Forms ($79)**
   - Basic contract templates
   - Change orders, lien notices
   - 5 most common states
   - Entry-level funnel product

2. **Professional ($149)** - PRIMARY TARGET
   - Everything in Essential
   - All 50 states covered
   - SBA funding guide
   - Proposal templates
   - "Most Popular" positioning

3. **Business System ($199)**
   - Everything in Professional
   - Job costing spreadsheets
   - AR/AP tracker
   - Lead tracking system
   - Priority email support

4. **Complete Bundle ($249)**
   - Everything in Business System
   - 3 state-specific deep dives
   - "Save $36" value prop

## State Resources Requirements

### Must Include (All 50 States):
- [ ] Licensing board name & URL
- [ ] License requirements (experience, exams, bonds)
- [ ] Lien law deadlines (preliminary notice, filing, foreclosure)
- [ ] Tax requirements (sales tax, contractor tax)
- [ ] Required document templates
- [ ] Quick links (Secretary of State, tax registration)

### Document Templates:
- [ ] Preliminary Notice
- [ ] Mechanics Lien
- [ ] Release of Lien
- [ ] Stop Notice
- [ ] Contract template (state-specific clauses)
- [ ] Change Order

## Automation Requirements

### Fully Automated:
- [ ] Purchase flow
- [ ] Payment processing
- [ ] Instant download delivery
- [ ] Email confirmation
- [ ] Post-purchase email sequence

### Human Intervention Only:
- Refund requests
- Technical issues (site down, broken links)
- Custom document requests
- Chargeback disputes
